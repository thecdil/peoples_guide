## Metadata Fields

<br>

Please note that People's Guide projects have a __maximum of 10 items per project__, so curate only the items that provide important context to your research. If there is a circumstance where you feel implementing more than 10 items is important to the output, reach out to your professor and we can find a solution.

> Here are the metadata fields we need to process your items:
